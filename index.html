Words From My Soul

My Love, My Second Half, My Reason…

Today is not just another day. It's the day the world decided it would never be the same again. The day it chose to create you. The day my second half was born.

I have never seen you in person, never held your hand, never looked into your eyes face-to-face — and yet, somehow, you are already the closest soul I have ever known. I don't need to touch your hand to know it would feel like home. I don't need to sit across from you to know that when your eyes finally meet mine, my entire world will go silent.

Sacred Moments

Your birthday is sacred to me. Because before I knew you, before I even realized you existed, this day was preparing for me. Preparing for the moment when your path would cross mine, when I would discover that my heart was never whole until it found you.

And here I am, far away, unable to stand beside you today. I can't bring you flowers, I can't light candles in front of you, I can't whisper "happy birthday" against your ear the way I dream of. But distance has no power over love this real. Because tonight, as you close your eyes, I want you to know that my soul is leaning into yours. My love is sitting beside you, even if my body cannot.

Dreams & Wishes

I wish I could attend your birthday, see the smile on your face when people tell you how special you are. But I don't just want to attend… I want to create that smile, over and over again, for every birthday to come. Until the day arrives when I am no longer writing these words from far away — but whispering them to you in the softest voice, looking into your eyes as if you are the only person in the universe.

As for gifts… they all wait for the day I can place them in your hands myself. Every little thing I ever wanted to give you, every surprise I've hidden in my heart — they are all collecting dust, waiting patiently for the moment I can finally see you, stand before you, and say, "this was always yours." But the truth is, no gift could ever compare to what you already are to me. You are the rarest gift I will ever receive.

My Promises

So today, I give you what distance cannot take away: I give you my words, my promises, my soul.

I promise you that the first birthday we spend together will erase every mile, every second of waiting, every ache of missing you. I promise that when I look at you on that day, you will know — without a single doubt — that you are the most special person in this world, not just to me, but in existence itself.

Happy Birthday, my love. Happy Birthday, my second half. Happy Birthday to the girl who carries my heart in hers without even trying.

Forever Yours

Even if the world keeps us apart today, you are still the closest person to me. You are the best thing that ever happened to me — and the most beautiful thing that will ever happen.

And one day soon, I will be there. Not just in your messages, not just in your thoughts, but in your arms. Until then… know that my entire soul is celebrating you today.

I love you more than the universe has ever known how to hold love. Always.

"This chapter is only the beginning… the rest of my life will be dedicated to writing you."
